# NNNN - Short title in the imperative or present tense

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** who made the call
- **Supersedes:** none <!-- or: NNNN - title -->
- **Superseded by:** none <!-- filled in later, if ever -->

## Context

What is the problem, force or constraint that makes a decision necessary? Describe the situation
neutrally - the technical, product and team factors in play - without yet naming the answer. State
the facts a future reader needs to judge whether this decision still holds.

## Decision

The choice, stated in the present tense as a standing instruction: "We use ...", "We adopt ...".
Be specific and unambiguous. If options were weighed, name the alternatives considered and why
they lost - briefly.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the costs and follow-up work we
are knowingly accepting, not just the upside. Note anything that would trigger revisiting this
decision.
